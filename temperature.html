Latitude & Temperature



Matplotlib Scatter Plot of Lat & Temp(F)

The date of this temperature snapshot is November 11th, 2018, so well into the northern hemisphere beginning to experience shorter days and colder temperatures (and obviously the opposite in the southern hemisphere).


Based on the data collected, the further north (greater latitude value) the colder the temperature!

Is this abnormal weather?

This isn't where you'll find any in depth information or arguments for or against global warming, but this image from the National Centers For Enviornmental Information is pretty interesting.

For most of the world, November 2017 temperatures were within +/-2 degrees Celsius from the previous ~30 years of weather data collected.

There are some extreme cases where the temperature is significantly (more than 3 Degrees Celsius) warmer or colder. The greatest departures from average temperatures are all located in the northern hemisphere.
