Data Source


All of our weather data was collected from OpenWeatherMap.org using an API to pull the data.
OpenWeatherMap.org collects weather data from around the globe including temperature, precipitation, wind speed and more.
Users are able to search for geospecific information using vary keywords or Geocoordinates.
For this assignment, we randomly generated no less than 500 longitude and Latitude locations and queried weather information from the nearest reporting station to our list of locations.
