Latitude Weather Analysis



Latitude:

in geography, latitude is a geographic coordinate that specifies the north-south position of a point on the Earth’s surface.

Image and definition are from wikipedia

Does Latitude impact weather conditions?

If we are just measuring average temperature, then the quick answer is the average, low, and high temperatures are probably impacted based on your proximity to the Equator. But what about other weather conditions such as wind speed, cloudiness, and humidity?


About this site:

If you found this site with the expectation of finding answers to your facinating list of questions about the relationship between latitude and weather conditions, I’m sorry to disappoint you but that’s not what your going to find here.

This site was developed as part of a school project for a Data Analytics and Visualization Bootcamp I’m taking at the University of Minnesota. We did set out to answer the question above, but really the point of the exercise was to use python to collect data using an API and visualize our findings with Matplotlib.

For more information what tools were used and the source of data, please visit our Data page.
